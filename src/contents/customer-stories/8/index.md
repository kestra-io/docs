##  About Copines de Voyage

Copines De Voyage is a unique travel agency dedicated to creating secure, intimate, and memorable travel experiences exclusively for women. With destinations that inspire and a community that connects, they turn simple trips into lifelong memories and friendships. To keep up with their growth and enhance their technological capabilities, Copines De Voyage partnered with Data Hanalytics to overhaul their data infrastructure, transitioning from a legacy PHP and cron job setup to a modern data stack that includes BigQuery, dbt, and Kestra.

## Identifying the Need for Change

The initiative to upgrade their data system was driven by the limitations of their existing setup, which included basic scheduling through cron jobs and a reliance on legacy PHP scripts. The growing IT team at Copines De Voyage sought a solution that could offer a centralized platform to manage increasingly complex data workflows. "We were growing fast and needed more structure," they explained, emphasizing the necessity for a more robust and scalable solution.

##Why Kestra Stood Out

Kestra was recommended by Data Hanalytics for its immediacy and simplicity. The introduction of Kestra marked a pivotal step towards simplifying data access and enhancing insight generation, which were critical for the team to simplify the onboarding of the team and to create data pipelines at scale. "Kestra is easy and offers great accessibility; we can see things quite quickly," noted a team member. The user-friendly interface and YAML-based configuration of Kestra made it a perfect fit for their needs, allowing for efficient and scalable workflow management.

## The Broad Impact of Kestra's Integration

Integrating Kestra into Copines De Voyage data ecosystem has been transformative. It facilitated the modernization of their data stack, allowing for streamlined workflow management. With Kestra, the team can now efficiently model data with dbt, ingest data into BigQuery more effectively, and achieve greater accuracy and utility from data parsed from APIs. This upgrade has improved all operations but also laid a robust foundation for continuous improvement and growth.

For instance, by using Kestra to automate the ingestion and processing of travel data, the team significantly reduced manual interventions, freeing up time to focus on more strategic initiatives. One team member remarked, "The experience with Kestra support is just awesome. I already convinced a friend to use Kestra for his data processes."

 ## Overcoming Legacy Challenges
 
Before Kestra, Copines De Voyage faced numerous challenges with their legacy system. The reliance on PHP scripts and cron jobs led to inefficiencies and a lack of scalability. The transition to a modern data stack was crucial. Now, with Kestra, they can automate and monitor data workflows more effectively, reducing errors and ensuring timely data processing.

## Future Directions

Looking ahead, Copines De Voyage plans to deepen their integration with current tools while exploring new technologies to enhance their data management capabilities further. They aim to refine data modeling, expand data ingestion sources, and develop more nuanced data parsing methods. The focus will be on optimizing analytics processes and ensuring they continue to deliver exceptional travel experiences.

## Conclusion

By adopting Kestra, Copines De Voyage has significantly advanced its data management capabilities, aligning technology with its mission to deliver unique and memorable travel experiences for women. 

> "Kestra has provided us with a robust platform to manage our data workflows efficiently. The support and accessibility have been fantastic."

Through this transformation, Copines De Voyage is well-positioned to continue providing outstanding travel experiences, supported by a modern, scalable, and efficient data infrastructure.
