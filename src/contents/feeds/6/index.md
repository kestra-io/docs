And it's packed with amazing features to make working with data even more delightful.
Here's what's new:
👍 Improved onboarding process for new users: Kestra now has a guided tour to introduce you to its main concepts.
👨‍💻 Better Developer Experience: Auto-completion directly in the editor, source code that's now preserved as you typed it with comments, and improved flow validation to catch errors before you save.
🛠 CI/CD support: Automate your flows' validation and deployment with GitHub Actions. We've also added support for exporting and importing flows and templates.
↕ Flow dependencies: See all the dependencies of a flow on the flow page tab.
✒ Mass actions everywhere: Delete multiple flows or executions at once with just one click!
And there's more!
