Vibe coding gets you to a prototype. Getting one into production is a different challenge: scheduling, retries, observability, and human-in-the-loop checkpoints before outputs get committed.

The agentic orchestration piece is what separates a prototype from something you can actually ship. LLM Zoomcamp is a free, 10-week course that will teach you how to build RAG pipelines and AI agents end-to-end, handle failures and retries, and monitor output quality in production.
