You love Kestra’s declarative orchestration, but as your usage grows, so do your requirements. How do you manage hundreds of users? How do you secure sensitive secrets? How do you scale across multiple clouds without downtime?

Join us for an exclusive walkthrough of Kestra Enterprise Edition. In this session, we will move beyond Kestra Core and dive into the infrastructure, security, and governance features designed for scaling your mission-critical operations.

Whether you are a Platform Engineer looking for High Availability or a Data Lead needing strict RBAC and Audit Logs, this webinar will demonstrate how Kestra Enterprise Edition transforms your orchestration into a secure, multi-tenant platform.

What you’ll learn:

- Improved Security: How to implement SSO, Secret Managers, and Audit Logs.
- Enterprise Governance: Mastering Multi-tenancy, Namespaces, Dependencies Graph, and RBAC.
- Infinite Scale: Using Worker Groups and Task Runners to run anywhere.
- Live Demo: A full tour of the Enterprise Interface.
