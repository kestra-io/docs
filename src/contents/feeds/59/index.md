In this post, we'll introduce CloudQuery, an open-source, high-performance ELT framework for developers. We'll briefly cover the product's history and its main strengths. We'll also look at a practical example of how to use CloudQuery to ingest data from Hacker News API to DuckDB, and how to put your CloudQuery syncs to production using Kestra.
