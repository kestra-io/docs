Kestra integrates with AWS! Our plugin provides seamless connectivity and functionality between Kestra and Amazon Web Services. You can now perform a range of data orchestration tasks on AWS with ease and efficiency.

💫 What you can do:

📦 S3: Upload, download, and manage files
🔀 SQS: Send, receive, and delete messages
🔔 SNS: Publish and manage notifications
🔺 DynamoDB: Perform operations on tables
